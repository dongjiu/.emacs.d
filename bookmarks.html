4.15.1 drop.exe

The VSTS Drop service is one of the Artifact Services being added to VSTS to support development workflows. Its main intent is to store, manage and make available build outputs. Typically this is output from CloudBuild but that is not a requirement. In the end a drop's content can come from any build system (Jenkins/Hudson, Mac, on-prem TFS Team Build, etc).
